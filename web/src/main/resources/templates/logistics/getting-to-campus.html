Check-in during conference weekend will be held at the Stephen Robert '62 Campus Center on the corner of Brown Street and Waterman Street. Please see the visitor's map attached here for more detailed information. 75 Waterman Street, Providence, RI 02912 is the best address to use for GPS navigation.

Providence is also easily accessible by both train and bus. Providence Station is served by Amtrak (Northeast Regional and Acela Express) and the MBTA Providence/Stoughton Line. Our campus is a short cab ride or a fifteen minute walk from the train station. Greyhound and Peter Pan bus lines stop at the Kennedy Plaza bus terminal, also a short cab ride or fifteen minute walk from campus. All of Rhode Island is served by the RIPTA public bus system, which is also accessible from Kennedy Plaza. The most accessible RIPTA bus stop on campus is located at the corner of Waterman Street and Thayer Street at the Thayer Street tunnel.

If your delegation is traveling from farther away, the closest airport to our campus is T.F. Green International Airport (PVD). T.F. Green is located in the city of Warwick, approximately 10 miles from Brown. Delegations traveling to campus from abroad may consider a number of transportation options from Boston Logan International Airport to Providence, including the MBTA commuter rail line and bus services mentioned above.

Please email info@busun.org with any transit-related questions.
